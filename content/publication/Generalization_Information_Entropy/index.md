abstract: From probability to mass function, by observing the evolution from Boltzmann entropy to Shannon entropy, Deng entropy and Random Permutation Set entropy, Generalization Information Dimension (GID) and Generalization Information Entropy (GIE) are proposed. In a closed system, GIE is equal to GID multiply bit numbers of state space. Both of Boltzmann entropy and GIE depend on number of states and increasing coefficient of corresponding system. Compared to previous information entropy, GIE is closer to thermodynamic entropy. The physical properties of GIE and GID bring many applications to information system especially method to calculate approximate value of entropy and avoid exponential explosion. GIE and GID provides a way to establish a strong connection between information theory and thermodynamics.
